Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2025

The Short Version

We don't collect anything. Everything stays on your device.

What We Don't Collect

  • Your typed words
  • Your corrections
  • Your usage patterns
  • Your device information
  • Your location
  • Your anything

What Stays On Your Device

Spellbound stores your personal dictionary and learned patterns locally on your iPhone. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

The 300MB Model

The FastText language model ships with the app. It runs entirely on-device. No API calls. No internet required.

Peer-to-Peer Learning (Optional)

If you enable Bluetooth mesh learning, Spellbound can share anonymized spelling patterns with nearby users via local Bluetooth. This is:

  • Opt-in only
  • Anonymous (no user IDs)
  • Local (device-to-device, no server)
  • Can be disabled anytime

Data Export

You can export a hash of your typing patterns. This hash is yours to keep, share, or sell. Spellbound takes no commission and has no access to where you send it.

No Analytics

This app contains no analytics, no crash reporting, no tracking pixels, and no third-party SDKs beyond the core iOS frameworks.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy (unlikely), we'll update this page and the app. But our core principle won't change: your words are yours.
