Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

The short version

Spellbound doesn't collect your data. We don't know who you are, what you type, or how you use the app. That's by design.

What we collect

Nothing. Zero. No analytics. No crash reporting. No telemetry. No third-party SDKs. No tracking pixels. No cookies. No server logs.

What stays on your device

Everything. Your typing patterns, corrections, learned words, and preferences are stored locally on your device. The 300MB FastText language model runs entirely on-device. Nothing is transmitted anywhere.

Peer-to-peer learning

If you opt in to Bluetooth mesh learning, anonymized pattern data is shared directly between nearby devices. This data never passes through any server. It's device-to-device only, and it's opt-in.

Data export & monetization

You can export your typing patterns as an anonymized hash. If you choose to sell this data through a future marketplace, you keep 100% of the revenue. We never access, process, or intermediate this data.

Third parties

There are none. No advertising networks. No analytics providers. No cloud services. No APIs. The app doesn't make network requests.

Children's privacy

Since we collect no data from anyone, we collect no data from children either.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, it will only become more private, never less. Any changes will be posted here.
