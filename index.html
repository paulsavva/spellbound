Spellbound

It knows your words. Not you.

A spell-checker that learns from you. On your device. Never leaves your phone.

No cloud. No tracking. No server. No third-party SDKs. No analytics. Nothing.

  • 300MB FastText model, runs entirely on-device
  • Learns your corrections locally, forgets what you ignore
  • Optional peer-to-peer learning via Bluetooth (anonymous)
  • Export your typing patterns as a hash. Sell it. Keep 100%.
